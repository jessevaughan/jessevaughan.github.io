ADDING NEW CONTENT

If you are the host of the group, follow these steps:

1. Tap top right icon (looks like three people standing together) when you're in your group to open right menu with Interests and Settings for the group

2. Tap the sports leagues you'd like to add to your group, choosing highlights and/or up to 3 teams to follow per league

3. Once you've added all the leagues you want, tap "Save" at the bottom of the screen

NOTE: It may take up to 1 min for the new picks to appear.

If you are not the host of the group, ask the host to add this content. You can also create a new group and add this content.

You can add as many new groups as you'd like.

1. Tap top right icon (looks like three people standing together) when you're in your group to open right menu with Interests and Settings for the group

2. Tap the "Create Group" button

3. Choose content channels

4. Name your new group

5. Invite friends to play along

NOTE: It may take up to 1 min for the new picks to appear.

We have 4 categories of notifications:

  • Picks available: letting you know Picks are available to be taken
  • 1 Hr Warnings: letting you know that the event is going to start in ~1 hour and you haven’t made any Picks
  • Post-Game Results: letting you know how you did on a game that just ended
  • Daily/Weekly Recap: letting you know how you did the previous day (or week)

Follow these steps to manage notifications:

  • Click on the top left corner icon (three lines - looks like a hamburger)
  • Click on "Manage Notifications"
  • Toggle your notification preferences on and off

Follow these steps to edit your profile:

  • Click on the top left corner icon (looks like a hamburger)
  • Click on your Handle or the pencil icon next to your Handle
  • Click on Handle, First Name or Last Name and edit as you see fit

Click Done in the top right hand corner when finished

Game

  • Doubles is a prediction games you play with your friends and family.
  • Doubles serves you Premise Cards on the morning of events that allow you to predict outcomes of live events taking place at a later time.
  • Swipe Premise Cards up (↑) or down (↓) to make (or change) picks. Picks are locked when events start.
  • +2 For each winning pick
  • +1 For each tie
  • -2 For each losing pick

--------------------------------------

  • x2 DOUBLE your score for any event when you make 5 or more picks without a loss

--------------------------------------

  • Leaderboard is reset weekly

We typically publish Picks the morning of an event, but sometimes the day before.

The user with the most points in your group at the end of the week wins the Crown.

You win the weekly Crown, bragging rights and the admiration/envy of all your friends.

Your score is DOUBLED for any event where you pick 5 (or more) Winners without a Loss.

There is no betting or gambling for money on Doubles.

At any time, you can view the current week's score as well as the prior week's score.
